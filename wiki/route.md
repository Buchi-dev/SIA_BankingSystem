### Routes

- **User Routes** (`/api/users`)
  - `POST /register`
  - `POST /login`
  - `GET /profile`
- **Account Routes** (`/api/accounts`)
  - `POST /create`
  - `GET /view/:id`
  - `PUT /update/:id`
  - `DELETE /delete/:id`
  - `GET /balance/:id`
- **Transaction Routes** (`/api/transactions`)
  - `POST /deposit`
  - `POST /withdraw`
  - `POST /transfer`
  - `GET /history`
- **External System Routes** (`/api/external-systems`)
  - `POST /register`
  - `POST /payment`
  - `GET /balance-inquiry`
