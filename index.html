Installation

You always have to install SIA Banking System on your client and your server. If you don't have it installed, you won't be able to use any of the system's features.

It doesn't matter which environment you are using. All of them are cross compatible.

If you have issues with these environments, visit their respective support channels for help:
  • Fabric
  • NeoForge
  • Forge
  • Spigot
  • Paper/Velocity/Waterfall
  • Quilt

Fabric

For the Fabric version, you need to have the Fabric Loader installed on your client/server.

Then copy the SIA Banking System mod to the mods folder.
